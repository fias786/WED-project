Earth Anthem
Our cosmic oasis, cosmic
blue pearl
the most beautiful planet in
the universe
all the continents and the
oceans of the world
united we stand as flora and fauna
united we stand as species of one earth
black, brown, white

"Earth Anthem" by Abhay K (Under CC by ND-NC)[9] On the occasion of the World Environment Day in June 2013, "Earth Anthem" by poet-diplomat Abhay K was launched at a function organized by the Indian Council of Cultural Relations in New Delhi by Kapil Sibal and Shashi Tharoor, Union Ministers of India.[10] It is in eight languages including all official languages of the United Nations which are Arabic, Chinese, English, French, Russian, Spanish.[11] The other two languages are Hindi and Nepali.
