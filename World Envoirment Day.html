World Envoirment Day

World Environment Day (W.E.D.) is celebrated every year on 5 June to raise global awareness to take positive environmental action to protect nature and the planet Earth. It is run by the United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP).

History
It was established by the United Nations General Assembly in 1972[1] on the day that United Nations Conference on the Human Environment began.
